WHO WE ARE?

RECLA is re-innovating the steel construction technologies that paves the new way for sustainable growth. Our company works under the impression of industry 5.0. We have an advantage of a globally and locally spread network. Along with in-house manufacturing facilities, our business model is designed to assist the local manufacturers to raise their products to international quality standards in order to aid the socio-economic conditions. By sharing the burden of huge capex, we have changed the way manufacturing industry operates. The aim is to provide one stop solution by executing the entire project with prompt services and best quality products. The company has evolved in creating excellence while delivering multiple construction types, such as – factories, multi-story risers, warehouses, commercial buildings, aircraft hangers and many more.
